## Description

Leader leads a whole song with a limited palette of movements. If the follower notices one of the excluded movements, she/he says: "Cheating!!" 

Sample constraints: 
* No crosses!
* No forward steps for follower
* No backward steps for the follower
* No crossed system
* No parallel system
